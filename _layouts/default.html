Research

I am broadly interested in security, programming languages, and machine learning. My recent research focuses on enhancing the security of AI-generated code, utilizing learning-based techniques that improve AI's security reasoning and formal methods that provide rigorous guarantees (see highlighted papers). I also work on related topics, including AI for programming and AI safety.
